About Wilson Galdamez

Hello! I'm Wilson, a 23-year-old enthusiast from Mount Kisco, having moved here from the vibrant streets of Brooklyn. My journey has been anything but ordinary, fueled by a lifelong passion for skateboarding, a keen interest in sports, and a dabbling in martial arts. Despite my active lifestyle, nothing compares to the joy I find in spending quality time with my family. Currently, I'm embracing the challenges and rewards of working full-time at Homegoods.

My dream of becoming a software engineer started in my childhood, a vision that has grown with me over the years. As I stand on the brink of my future, eager and optimistic, I look forward to the opportunities and adventures it holds for me. Join me on this exciting journey as I transition from the retail aisles of Homegoods to the innovative avenues of software engineering.

You can find me on GitHub or connect with me on LinkedIn at LinkedIn. The best way to reach out to me is via email or LinkedIn message. Can't wait to see what the future has in store!
